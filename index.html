About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

Shri Dasharath Industrial Training Institute is dedicated to shaping skilled professionals and contributing to the nation's technical and holistic healthcare workforce. Under the guidance of Director Neelam Pundora and the leadership of Principal Ajay Prakash Pundora, the institute emphasizes hands-on learning, discipline, and industry-relevant training. We offer a diverse range of certified programs in Electrician, Fitter, Solar Technician, Fire Technology, and Industrial Safety Management. In addition to technical education, we also offer specialized healthcare and wellness programs such as Ayurvedic Pharmacy, D.Pharma (Ayurveda), and Yog & Prakritik Chikitsa Sahaya.

At Shri Dasharath Industrial Training Institute, we are committed to empowering students with the knowledge, practical skills, and confidence needed to build successful careers and contribute meaningfully to society.
